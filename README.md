BIZNET - Find Beauty Businesses You Can Trust
Hey! Welcome to Biznet. This is a mobile app that helps you find real beauty businesses and read honest reviews from actual customers.

What's Biznet All About?
You know that feeling when you're scrolling on Instagram looking for a new beauty product, but you don't know if the seller is legit? Yeah, that's the problem Biznet solves.

For Customers:

Search for beauty businesses selling what you want (lipstick, skincare, etc.)
Read real reviews from people who actually bought from them
See clear star ratings (no fake stuff)
Write your own review after you buy something
Share photos or videos of your purchase
For Business Owners:

Get a store page on Biznet
See what customers say about your business
Keep track of your rating
Manage your products
See trends in your reviews
It's basically like Google Reviews but just for African beauty businesses.

How to Get Started
Install the App
Download Biznet from Google Play Store (search "Biznet")
Open the app
Sign up with your email
Choose if you're a customer or business owner
Start using!
For Customers
First time?

Tap "Search" at the bottom
Type what you're looking for ("MAC lipstick", "skincare", etc.)
See all the businesses that sell it
Tap one to see their reviews
Read what others said
Click on their store link to go buy
Want to leave a review?

After you buy, go to that business's page
Tap "Write Review"
Give it stars (1-5)
Write what you think (at least 20 characters, be honest!)
Add a photo if you want
Hit submit
Your profile:

See all the reviews you've written
Edit or delete your reviews anytime
Update your profile picture
For Business Owners
Getting started:

Sign up as a "business owner"
Fill in your business info (name, description, logo)
Add the link to where people can actually buy (Instagram, Shopify, etc.)
Add your products (what you sell)
Done! You're live
Using your dashboard:

See your average rating
See how many reviews you have
Read recent reviews from customers
Check your analytics (see trends)
Reply to reviews if you want
Managing your stuff:

Add new products anytime
Update your business info
See which products customers mention most
Track your rating over time
How We Keep It Real
We want Biznet to actually help people, so we have some rules:

You have to use your real email to leave a review
Reviews stay even if they're negative (we don't delete honest feedback)
Business owners can't delete reviews (only if they're spam)
If we see fake reviews, we remove them
We calculate ratings the honest way (no tricks)
What Happens if You Don't Have Internet?
No wifi? No problem (sort of).

If your internet goes down:

You can still browse businesses and reviews you already looked at (we save them)
You can't post new reviews (need internet for that)
You can't see new businesses or up-to-date ratings
But when internet comes back, everything updates automatically
We show a little "You're offline" message at the top so you know what's happening.

Technical Stuff (If You Care)
Built with:

Flutter (so it works great on Android and will work on iPhone too)
Firebase (keeps all your data safe and backed up)
Firestore (the database that powers searches)
Your data is safe because:

You log in with email/password (encrypted)
Your reviews are linked to you, but your personal info stays private
Business owners can only edit their own stuff
We follow Google's security rules for protecting data
What Can Go Wrong and How to Fix It
"I forgot my password"

Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen
Enter your email
Check your email for the reset link
Done!
"The app is slow"

Close and reopen it
Make sure you have good internet
If it's still slow, try restarting your phone
"I can't upload a photo"

Make sure the photo file isn't too big (under 5MB)
Check your internet connection
Try again
"I see an error message"

If it says "Something went wrong," just try again
If it keeps happening, restart the app
Still broken? Let us know (feedback button in settings)
"My review didn't post"

Make sure you have internet
Make sure you wrote at least 20 characters
Make sure you gave it a star rating
Try again

Just Starting Out as a Developer?
If you're looking at the code:

File structure:

lib/screens/ — All the pages (home, search, reviews, etc.)
lib/services/ — How we talk to Firebase
lib/providers/ — How data flows around the app
lib/models/ — What a business, review, user looks like
lib/widgets/ — Reusable UI pieces (buttons, cards, etc.)
Installing and running:

flutter pub get
flutter run
Testing:

flutter test
Building for real:

flutter build apk --release
